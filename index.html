Create a healthy portfolio-life balance

When you know where you stand, you can focus on the present moments in your life that matter, and worry less about the daily machinations of the stock market.

See your allocations across multiple accounts

With your investments (and your spouse's) spread across multiple accounts or even institutions, it's hard to see how it all fits together. Allocation buckets make it super clear.

Update your asset values on your time

No more fiddling with glitchy syncing or distractions with daily price movements. Just enter your assets and update their current values on a calm schedule that works for you.

Know how much money you need to move to keep things in balance

With multiple accounts and different tax treatments, rebalancing is not as simple as clicking a button. Seeing differences in dollars between your actual and target bucket allocations lets you know what to move where.
